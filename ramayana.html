The Ramayana

  1. Excellent English translations of the Ramayana range from faithful, unabridged epics to accessible modern retellings.
  2. The Ramayana, also known as the Valmiki Ramayana, as traditionally attributed to Valmiki, is a Hindu smriti epic poem from ancient India.
